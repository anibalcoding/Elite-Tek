Founder & Head Coach

Nico Zaldana

Nico Zaldana is the Founder and Head Coach of Elite Tek Soccer Academy, bringing years of experience developing elite youth players in the Dallas area. His approach is built on technical precision, intentional repetition, and the belief that every player has untapped potential waiting to be unlocked.

Under his guidance, players have improved their first touch, accelerated their decision-making, and earned spots on higher-level club teams. His training goes beyond drills — it builds the mindset required to compete at the next level.
